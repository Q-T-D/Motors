Understanding Japanese Auction Grading

Japanese auction houses use a comprehensive grading system to evaluate vehicle condition.

5/A
Excellent Condition

Like new with no visible defects or repairs needed.

4.5/B
Very Good Condition

Minor wear and tear, no major issues.

3.5/C
Good Condition

Some repairs needed but mechanically sound.

2/D
Fair Condition

Needs repairs, may have accident history.
